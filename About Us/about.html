About Us

Welcome to Dangal, your go-to store for authentic Philippine native products. We take pride in showcasing the rich culture and craftsmanship of local artisans, offering handmade items that tell a story of tradition and heritage. From woven fabrics to handcrafted home decor, every piece is made with passion and skill. Join us in celebrating Filipino artistry, one product at a time.

Our Mission

Dangal Corporation strives to create a vibrant and sustainable marketplace that connects consumers with unique, locally-sourced goods, benefiting both businesses and communities for years to come. To forge a direct connection between Filipino artisans and the unique, locally-sourced goods they create. Dangal Corporation aims to achieve this by providing a sustainable and ethical marketplace that benefits both businesses and the communities they represent. This includes offering fair pricing structures, transparent supply chains. Beyond facilitating transactions, our platform aims to build a thriving online community. This will be a space where artisans can connect with each other, share knowledge and best practices, and collaborate on projects. It will also be a place where consumers can discover new and exciting products, learn about the rich heritage of Filipino craftsmanship, and engage directly with the creators.

Vision

To provide consumers with the premier online destination for discovering and purchasing unique, handcrafted goods, celebrating Filipino artistry and supporting local economies. Dangal Corporation aims to be more than just an e-commerce site; striving to cultivate a vibrant ecosystem that fosters economic empowerment and ignites cultural pride. This means providing a space where Filipino artisans and businesses can not only sell their goods but also share their stories, connect with their customers, and build lasting relationships. Dangal Corporation envisioned a future where Filipino craftsmanship is celebrated globally through a website that serves as a powerful engine for sustainable growth and development within Filipino communities.
