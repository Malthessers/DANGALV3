Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to questions you may have. Can't find what you're looking for? come Contact Us!

Is there a free trial available? ▼
Yes, you can try us for free for 30 days. If you want, we'll provide you with a free 30-minute onboarding call to get you up and running. Our trial allows you to explore all features without commitment, ensuring our services align with your needs.
Can I change my plan later? ▼
Of course! Our pricing scales with your company. Chat to our friendly team to find a solution that works for you as you grow. You can upgrade, downgrade, or modify your plan anytime to accommodate business changes.
What is your cancellation policy? ▼
We understand that things change. You can cancel your plan at any time and we'll refund you the difference already paid. We aim to make our cancellation process hassle-free, ensuring flexibility for our customers.
How does billing work? ▼
Plans are per workspace, not per account. You can upgrade one workspace and still have any number of free workspaces. Each workspace is billed separately, allowing for better cost management.
Can other info be added to an invoice? ▼
At the moment, the only way to add additional information to invoices is to manually add the information to the workspace's name. However, we are working on features to allow more customization.
How do I change my account email? ▼
You can change the email address associated with your account by going to untitled.com/account from a laptop or desktop. Ensure your new email is verified to maintain account security.
What happens if I exceed my usage limit? ▼
If you exceed your usage limit, you'll receive an email notification and have the option to upgrade your plan or reduce your usage. We offer additional packages to accommodate growing needs.
Do you offer customer support? ▼
Yes! Our support team is available 24/7 via email and chat to help you with any issues or questions. We strive to provide fast, efficient, and helpful assistance whenever needed.
